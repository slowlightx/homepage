About Me

Howdy! I am a Physics PhD student from Department of Physics and Astronomy, Rice University. I am doing computational condensed matter physics, with a focus on solving frustrated magnetism problems using tensor network states. Please see Research page for more details about my research interests. In my leisure time, I enjoy watching and playing soccer, running, bicycling, playing ping-pong, watching anime, and photography.
